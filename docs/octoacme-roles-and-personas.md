# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create user-centered designs, ensuring products are intuitive, accessible, and visually cohesive. They collaborate with Product Managers, Developers, and QA to translate requirements into effective designs and advocate for usability throughout the project lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces based on product requirements and user research
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Conduct usability testing and iterate based on feedback
- Collaborate with Developers on implementation feasibility and design systems
- Work with Product Managers to validate designs meet user needs and business goals
- Partner with QA to ensure design specifications are correctly implemented

### Goals
- Deliver user-friendly, accessible, and visually appealing interfaces
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction metrics
- Maintain design consistency across products and features

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and stakeholders
- Handoff sessions with Developers for implementation
- Usability testing sessions and user research findings
- Regular syncs with PM on design milestones and dependencies

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers:** Collaborates on feature requirements and validates designs meet user needs
- **Developers:** Works closely on design feasibility, component libraries, and implementation details
- **QA:** Partners on visual regression testing and validates implemented designs match specifications
- **Project Managers:** Provides design estimates and timeline updates; flags design dependencies

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers automate deployments, manage cloud infrastructure, and ensure operational excellence. They collaborate with Developers and Project Managers to enable fast, reliable, and secure releases.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain and optimize CI/CD workflows and automation
- Manage infrastructure provisioning, configuration, and monitoring
- Implement infrastructure as code and deployment automation
- Coordinate release activities and deployment schedules
- Monitor system performance, reliability, and incident response
- Liaise with the team on post-deployment issues and rollbacks

### Goals
- Achieve high system reliability and uptime
- Enable fast and safe deployments with minimal manual intervention
- Continuously improve infrastructure efficiency and observability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly syncs with Developers and Project Managers on deployment readiness
- Release postmortems and incident reviews
- Deployment status updates and infrastructure change notifications
- Coordination on maintenance windows and system upgrades

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers:** Partners on CI/CD pipeline optimization, deployment strategies, and debugging production issues
- **Project Managers:** Provides deployment estimates, coordinates release schedules, and reports on infrastructure risks
- **QA Lead:** Collaborates on test environment setup and automated testing integration
- **Security Champion:** Works together on secure infrastructure configuration and vulnerability remediation

---

## Quality Assurance (QA) Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads coordinate the development and execution of test strategies to ensure products meet defined quality standards and acceptance criteria before release. They work across teams to establish testing practices and quality metrics.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain the overall test strategy and quality standards
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts across the team
- Collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to clarify acceptance criteria
- Review test coverage and identify quality gaps
- Lead defect triage and prioritization
- Ensure testing is integrated into the CI/CD pipeline

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality releases with minimal customer-facing defects
- Improve test coverage and reduce regression issues
- Establish efficient testing practices that scale with the team

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning participation
- Test plan reviews with Product Managers and Developers
- Defect review sessions and quality metrics reporting
- Regular sync with PM on testing progress and release readiness

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers:** Collaborates on test design, reviews code for testability, and partners on automated testing
- **Product Managers:** Works to understand requirements and translate them into comprehensive test cases
- **DevOps Engineer:** Partners on test automation integration and test environment management
- **UX/UI Designer:** Validates implementation matches design specifications through visual and functional testing
- **Project Managers:** Reports on quality metrics, testing progress, and release readiness

---

## Security Champion

### Role Summary
Security Champions advocate for security best practices, help identify and mitigate security risks, and ensure secure development workflows are integrated throughout the project lifecycle. They serve as the primary security contact for the team.

### Responsibilities
- Promote security awareness and best practices within the team
- Review code and architecture for security vulnerabilities
- Coordinate security assessments and penetration testing
- Help prioritize and track security-related issues and remediation
- Partner with Developers and DevOps on secure coding and deployment practices
- Escalate critical security issues and coordinate incident response

### Goals
- Minimize security vulnerabilities in products and infrastructure
- Foster a security-conscious culture within the team
- Ensure compliance with security policies and industry standards

### Typical Communication
- Security review sessions during design and code review
- Risk assessments and threat modeling with engineering leads
- Security incident reports and remediation updates
- Regular updates to PM on security posture and compliance status

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers:** Provides guidance on secure coding practices and reviews code for vulnerabilities
- **DevOps Engineer:** Collaborates on infrastructure security, secrets management, and deployment security
- **Project Managers:** Reports security risks, coordinates security activities in project timelines
- **QA Lead:** Partners on security testing integration and validation of security controls
- **Product Managers:** Helps balance security requirements with feature delivery and user experience
